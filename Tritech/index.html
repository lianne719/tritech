About Us

Tritech Sdn Bhd, was established in 1982, is Malaysia’s leading manufacturer and distributor for high visibility garments and safety products for use on roads, highway and work-sites. Tritech offers a wide range of innovative high visibility garments and products that conform to international standards and the products can be custom made to meet customers’ needs and requirements.

Tritech has been a distributor for major international brands including Reflexite (U.S.A.) microprism retroreflective sheeting, Peter Berghaus (Germany), Sparkler (South Korea), Tildawn (England), Mechatronic (Germany), Astro Optics (U.S.A.), Superior Lightings (U.S.A.), Haenni (Switzerland), AutoTest (Australia) and many other renowned brands and quality products.

Tritech is fully committed to providing continuous innovations, quality products and best service to our customers.
